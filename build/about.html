What Is The Cryptocoin Development Index?


We are like you. We spend our hard earned money on cryptocurrencies to make money. We dove in headfirst and attempted to disect which cryptocurrencies would make us potential money. At first it was simple. There were not many coins available to choose from. Then ICO's exploded and now we have hundreds of cryptocurrencies available to invest in. What bothered us was the lack of transparency around ICO's and the development teams behind these cryptocurrencies.

Something that we noticed more often as the cryptocurrency markets exploded was the lack of information surrounding the software development of their respective blockchains. Our aim of the crypto coin software development index is to provide transparency on:

  • Core software development of crypto coin blockchains
  • Development process and obstacles facing crypto coin teams
  • Size and technical ability of the crypto coin development teams
  • Developer community and developer jobs associated with crypto coin teams

In a nutshell, what are we all about? We aim to cut through all the marketing fluff that you might encounter. Can a crypto coin with a development team of three developers really be worth hundreds of millions of dollars? Time will tell, in the mean time we aim to track all software development related progress of all crypto coins in our coin developer index.
